Send a message on any phone

All you do is send a message to the wheeltalks phone number. Include a license plate and a message and that message will be there waiting for that driver.

Protect your car

Wheeltalks helps you stay connected with you car by allowing people to notify you when your car is in any sort of danger. Know when you're blocked in, when your car alarm is going off, or when you're at risk of getting towed.

Help other people

Notify someone when their car is in danger. Wheeltalks is perfect for telling someone when they are at risk of getting towed, their car alarm is going off, they have a flat tire or are parked illegally.

See what people have been saying

Click "get connected" and receive an email of all the messages that have been sent to your license plate. Make sure you're driving well, your car is safe and everyone on the road is wishing you well!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What happens if someone misuses wheeltalks?

    We reserve the right to block people from the wheeltalks service

  • Is there a wheeltalks app?

    No. Wheeltalks is a service based around text messaging. Apps are in production to help serve particular members of the community.

  • Will my contact information be shared with anyone?

    Absolutely not. Anonyminity is an important part of Wheeltalks.

  • How secure is Wheeltalks

    User information is kept in a secure couchDB database.

  • How will I know if my message has reached the car owner?

    You will receive a message saying your message has been sent to the car owner.
